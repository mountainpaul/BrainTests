PAL is a touchscreen-based test. The participant sees several boxes (usually 2, 4, 6, or 8) arranged around the screen.

General test flow:
	1.	A box opens and briefly shows a simple pattern (visual abstract shape).
	2.	The box closes.
	3.	After all patterns have been shown in their correct boxes, the screen displays one pattern at a time in the center.
	4.	The participant must touch the box where that pattern was originally shown.

The test automatically increases difficulty based on performance.
